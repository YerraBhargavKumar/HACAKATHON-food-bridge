# 🍱 Food Bridge – Leftover Food Initiative

Food Bridge is a simple web platform designed to reduce food wastage by connecting people who have excess food with those who need it.

---

## 🚀 Idea

Every day, a lot of food gets wasted while many people go hungry.  
Food Bridge tries to solve this problem by acting as a bridge between:

- 🍽️ Food donors (restaurants, individuals, events)
- 🤝 Volunteers
- 🏠 People in need

---

## 🛠️ Features

- 📍 Location-based food donation
- 📝 Simple donation form
- 👥 Volunteer support
- 📢 Awareness about food wastage
- 📱 Responsive UI design
